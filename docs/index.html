An Adventure Into the Computer

Join a heroic cursor on his epic journey through computer hardware in our 2D top-down game. Throughout the journey you will meet and save various characters and gain powers that no antivirus ever dreamed of. Save the computer and destroy the nasty virus.

The game is played with a keyboard. After a brief introduction to the game’s story the player starts the game playing as a cursor in starting tutorial area (the CPU). From there they proceed to the first level. At first they don’t have any special abilities. The cursor can only do a basic dash attack. By playing the first level the player obtains the first special ability - shooting. Most of the abilities that the player can acquire are used for solving puzzles and fighting enemies.

Each level has a puzzle, where the player will need to use their newly acquired ability. Each level has a character symbolizing an important computer icon.
